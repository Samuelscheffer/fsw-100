The Beginning

It all Started a on a misty August morning in Rural Kansas, on The 30th day of August in the year 1984 A.D. Scott Andrew Scheffer Was born on that misty day, as he was born a mysterious smell filled the air... The smell of smoke, the aromas of Mesquite, Hickory, Maple and many others filled the hospital ward. It is rumored Even at that age his sausage was the best tasting around.
